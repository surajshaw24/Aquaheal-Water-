Our Story

Welcome to Aquaheal Water — a brand born from Dhanbad, built with honesty, hard work, and the dream of purity in every drop.

Founded by Suraj Shaw, a young B.Com graduate who believed that success isn't just about degrees — it's about creating something meaningful. With that thought, Aquaheal Water was started to deliver pure, safe, and healthy water to every home.

"Har ghar tak shuddh paani, har dil tak sehat."

Every bottle of Aquaheal reflects dedication, trust, and care. Our advanced purification process ensures that every drop you drink is free from impurities and enriched with essential minerals for your health.

Suraj Shaw

Founder, Aquaheal Water
